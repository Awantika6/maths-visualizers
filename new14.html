The Matrix Operations

×

Multiplication

The "Combiner." It calculates the interaction between two sets of data by multiplying rows of the first by columns of the second.

T

Transpose

The "Reorienter." It flips a matrix over its diagonal. Rows become columns, and columns become rows.

|A|

Determinant

The "Scale Factor." A single number that tells you if the matrix expands, shrinks, or collapses the area of an object.

−1

Inverse

The "Undo Button." It reverses a transformation. It only exists if the Determinant is not zero.
